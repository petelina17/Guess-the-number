Welcome!

This is a game where you try to guess a number between 1 and 100.

The computer will generate a random number and you and your opponents will try to guess for the right number.

After you guess a number, the computer will tell you if your number is higher or lower than.

When Player one's turn is over it jump to the next player and continue that way until one person guesses right.

You can choose to fight either one or two opponents

The opponents can either be a computer controlled AI or your own friends.

Be sure to have fun!
